Frequently Asked Questions about Car Rental in Goa

What is the cheapest car rental option near Dabolim Airport?

Our Maruti Ignis and Swift models are the most affordable options starting at ₹1,200 per day. Both are available in manual and automatic transmissions. We offer complimentary airport pickup from Dabolim International Airport with all our bookings. Book directly via WhatsApp for instant confirmation.

How do I book a car rental in Goa with GoaRide?

Booking is simple and quick! You can book through our website by filling the booking form or directly via WhatsApp at +91 8262812997. Simply select your vehicle, pickup date, and location. We'll send you a confirmation and all details instantly via WhatsApp.

What documents do I need to rent a car in Goa?

You'll need a valid driving license, ID proof (Aadhar/Passport), and a refundable security deposit. The deposit ranges from ₹3,000 for economy cars to ₹10,000 for luxury vehicles. This is returned in full upon safe return of the vehicle.

Do you offer car rental near Dabolim Airport with pickup service?

Yes! We offer complimentary airport pickup and drop-off service at Dabolim International Airport for all car rentals. Just mention your flight details and preferred pickup time during booking. Our representative will meet you at the airport with your car.

Are your rental cars fully insured?

Absolutely! All our vehicles come with comprehensive insurance coverage. The insurance is included in the rental rate, ensuring you're fully protected during your rental period in Goa. We're committed to your safety and peace of mind.

What is included in the car rental price?

Our rental prices include: comprehensive insurance, GPS navigation system, 24/7 roadside assistance, and airport/hotel pickup and drop-off service. There are no hidden charges. What you see is exactly what you pay. Additional charges only apply for extra kilometers beyond the package limit.
